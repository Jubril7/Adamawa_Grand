Our Story

About Adamawa Grand Hotel & Suites

A premier destination for comfort, culture and connection in the capital of Adamawa State.

Yola's Home of Hospitality

Adamawa Grand Hotel & Suites was founded with a single vision: to bring world-class hospitality to the historic city of Yola, the capital of Adamawa State in northeastern Nigeria.

Situated at the confluence of the River Benue and its tributaries, Yola is a city of rich history, diverse cultures and stunning natural landscapes. Our hotel draws inspiration from this vibrant heritage, blending contemporary comfort with authentic warm hospitality.

Every member of our team is committed to ensuring your stay is memorable — from our locally sourced cuisine to our meticulously maintained rooms and our professional, courteous service.
